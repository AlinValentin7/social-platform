

## Utente
- id BIGINT UNSIGNED PK AUTO UNIQUE NOTNULL
- name CHAR(60) NOTNULL
- lastname CHAR(60) NOTNULL
- age DATE NOTNULL
- email VARCHAR(255) NOTNULL UNIQUE
- password CHAR (80) NOTNULL
- nickname CHAR(80) NOTNULL UNIQUE 
- gender CHAR (20) NULL
